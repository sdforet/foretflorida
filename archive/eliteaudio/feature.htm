Theo Kalomirakis

Theo Kalomirakis is widely regarded as the father of professional home theater designs. He has won numerous awards for his classy and inventive designs, and is rumored to have developed the personal theaters of the most famous people in the world today. His designs blend a classic elegance with a modern functionality like no other designer before. Whether your looking for hyper traditional theater design with a classic proscenium, or an ultra modern space-age theme theater with cool blue accents like the one shown to the left, Theo is the man.
