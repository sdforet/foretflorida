About Sean

Thank you for taking the time to browse over my work and I hope that your search for the right developer is going well.

I am originally from South Louisiana, and although my accent is gone, I am still a Cajun. I have many hobbies, which you can browse through on my primary site. The two that bring me the most enjoyment is music (playing bass/piano), and horsepower. There are few experiences that can compete with driving a 450HP 5.0L V8 off the line.

I now live in Newberry with my family and have been working fulltime as a Software Developer for Gleim Internet since April of 2013. This position requires proficiency with the following:

  • PHP
  • PHPTAL
  • Symfony
  • Doctrine
  • TWIG
  • jQuery
  • JavaScript
  • MySql
  • Notepad++ (primary editor)
  • Version Control using Git and Mercurial
  • MinTTY with GitBash
  • gitHub
  • gitLab
  • PuTTY
  • VIM
  • Mozilla Developers Tools

I try not to take myself too seriously. I am a good communicator and enjoy contributing to a professional, fun, and engaging environment. I am loyal and committed to the core, and would love the opportunity to continue cultivating my abilities with your team.
