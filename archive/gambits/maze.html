Maze Game

Welcome to the Maztrix... Not really. Here is just a simple maze game using HTML 5 elements and a little nostalgic controls thrown in for effect. You can use the keyboard's number pad (if so equiped)
8=up | 2=down | 4=left | 6=right
Control the green square with the number pad and guide it through the maze or you can use the d-pad on the SF controller. The start button resets the board and your position. Have fun!
