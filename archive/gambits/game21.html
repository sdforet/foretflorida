Game of 21

Play the game of twenty one, the rules are simple:

  • Hit your number until get as close to twenty one as possible without going over.
  • The computer will then play and try to beat you.
  • If both players go over 21, then no one wins.
  • If neither player goes over, the player closest to 21 wins the round.
  • If both are tied then no one wins it is a tie.

Good luck, may the best man/machine win!
