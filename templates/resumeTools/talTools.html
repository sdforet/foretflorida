PHPTAL

PHPTAL is a template engine for PHP. It has many benefits such as: separation of logic and presentation, a robust macro system, no speed loss, fails with helpful warnings for the developer, and forces production of clean XHTML. These and many other advantages of PHPTAL are listed on the group's site: PHPTAL.org.

/**
 * the "new" object will identify
 * an inner template passed as an
 * argument, which is inserted into
 * a parent template identified by
 * the class
 */
require_once "php/Decorator.php";
$page = new Decorator('myPage.xhtml');
/**
 * any business logic will reside
 * here and get passed to the
 * page object
 */
echo $page->execute();
